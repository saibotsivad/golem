Another topic that stands on its own: attention visualization — extracting the attention weight matrices from GPT-2 and rendering them as a heatmap showing which tokens attend to which. This is the core mechanism that makes transformers different from prior architectures, and it's visually striking. It requires digging into the model internals a bit more (Transformers.js can return attention weights if the ONNX export includes them), but it's the most "look inside the black box" moment in the whole series.
